Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal data and cookies

Introduction

At Danis Photography, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through our contact form, including:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Message content

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Provide photography services
  • Communicate with you about bookings and sessions

Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the website functions properly. These cookies are necessary for the site to work and cannot be switched off.

With your consent, we may also use analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our website. You can accept or decline these cookies through our cookie consent banner.

Data Storage and Security

Your personal information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

Changes to This Policy
